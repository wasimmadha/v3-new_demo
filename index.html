Improvements in Lightning V3

Experience the latest enhancements in Lightning V3. This release focuses on sounding more natural, staying stable under varied inputs, and conveying intent with greater clarity and control.

  • Natural prosody: Human-like rhythm, stress, and intonation across phrases and sentences.
  • Better pause handling: Punctuation-aware pacing that adds natural breaks and avoids run-on delivery.
  • Robust stability: Fewer artifacts and steadier performance on long or complex prompts.
  • Emotional nuance: Clearer expression of intent and emotion without mentioning file names.
  • Consistent amplitude: Stable loudness levels with reduced sudden spikes or dips.
  • Richer paralinguistics: Appropriate use of breaths, hesitations, and non-lexical cues.
