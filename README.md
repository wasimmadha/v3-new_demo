# Lightning V3 Demo

A clean demonstration of the Lightning V3 voice AI system showcasing natural prosody, better pause handling, robust stability, emotional nuance, consistent amplitude, and richer paralinguistics.

## Features

- **Natural prosody**: Human-like rhythm, stress, and intonation across phrases and sentences
- **Better pause handling**: Punctuation-aware pacing that adds natural breaks and avoids run-on delivery
- **Robust stability**: Fewer artifacts and steadier performance on long or complex prompts
- **Emotional nuance**: Clearer expression of intent and emotion
- **Consistent amplitude**: Stable loudness levels with reduced sudden spikes or dips
- **Richer paralinguistics**: Appropriate use of breaths, hesitations, and non-lexical cues

## Voice Samples

The demo includes 17 voice samples showcasing various aspects of the Lightning V3 system, from emotional expressions to technical stability tests.

## Usage

Simply open `index.html` in a web browser to explore the voice samples and experience the Lightning V3 capabilities.
